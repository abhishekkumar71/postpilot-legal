Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: Sep 23, 2025

If you would like PostPilot to delete any personal data we hold about you, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at marsakatlaabhishek7168@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In your message, include the email address associated with your PostPilot account and a short description of the data you want deleted.
  3. We will respond within 7 business days with confirmation and next steps.

What we will delete

On verification we will delete user profile data stored by PostPilot, associated Page/Instagram tokens, and any scheduled posts that are unpublished. We will not be able to delete data stored by third-party platforms (Facebook, Instagram) — you must delete those from those services directly.
