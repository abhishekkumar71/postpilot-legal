Privacy Policy

Last updated: Sep 23, 2025

PostPilot ("we", "us", "our") operates the PostPilot app and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in via Facebook, we may receive your name, email address and profile information.
  • Page and Instagram data: With your permission we may receive Page IDs, Instagram Business Account IDs, access tokens and media you authorize us to post on your behalf.
  • Content you create: Post captions, image URLs, scheduled dates and related metadata.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and authorize posting on your Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts.
  • To schedule, create, publish and manage posts you request through the app.
  • To improve and maintain the app, and for security and fraud prevention.

Sharing & Third Parties

We will never sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help host or run the app (for example cloud hosting or analytics). For posting to social networks we act as an intermediary and use access tokens you authorize — we do not share your tokens with unrelated third parties.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account exists or as necessary to provide the services. You can request deletion (see below).

Your Choices

You can disconnect your social accounts from the app at any time; you may also request data deletion by emailing us (see contact below).

Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure — please contact us if you suspect any issue.
